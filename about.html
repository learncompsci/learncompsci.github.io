About

learncompsci.org was inially created by Ben Greenberg and Simon Mahns as a way to share the curricula that was created by ORION. Now, learncompsci.org is a simple "hub" where computer science learning materials can be easily shared and viewed. Feel free to use these materials for your robotics team, coding clubs, etc.
